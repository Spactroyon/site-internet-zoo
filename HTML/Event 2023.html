The event of 2023

The zoological park of Paris or Zoo de Vincennes is a zoo that you can visit with your family, and learn as much as possible about these animals.
There is 6 zones very distinct and faithful to the natural environments and habitats of the animals.


discover the map of the park



Event program



Our team, made up of 4 people, went to the zoo Saturday, April 8, 2023.
We went to this zoo because it offered predator season which is a period when hunters become the stars of the zoo. Thus each predator was presented with its talent in nature like the Eurasian lynx which is discreet or the jaguar with its steel jawbone. 20 candidates are selected and we could vote for our favorites.

But there was above all a predator that made us come to this zoo, it's the ditch.

The fossa is a feliform mammal of the family Eupleridae. In the park there is a male and a female and recently the male was exchanged with another from Germany.
This exchange will allow the fossas to to reproduce, again, as the old fossa male had given birth with the female still in the park three babies.
This species is in danger of extinction and this is the reason for the presence of this animal in the zoo, protecting them from external dangers and making them reproduce, the fossa will be saved.

For the welfare of the fossas, stimulation is needed, to do this, one can stimulate any of their senses. For example, view by modifying the locations of certain elements of the cage or the sense of smell with different aromas.
