Presentation of our team



We are a team of 4 people, Nathan Boulangeot, Alexandre Meunier, Jawed Bensaih et Farès Nassim, which premiered on March 10, 2023.
  • Nathan Boulangoet:
    • Chef du groupe : Nathan is the leader of the group, responsible for coordinating activities and making decisions. He also ensures communication between the members and the overall management of the project.
    • Photographer : Nathan est compétent en photographie, chargé de capturer des images lors de l'événement ou des activités connexes. Il peut être responsable de la documentation visuelle du groupe.
    • Designer and translator : Nathan has design skills, which allows him to contribute to the graphic creation of the website or other visual aids. He also ensures the creation of the event poster.
  • Alexandre Meunier:
    • Find event information : Alexandre is responsible for gathering relevant information on the event in question. He carries out extensive research to obtain accurate and useful data, which can be used in the design of the website or in other areas of the project.
    • CSS/JS/HTML programming of the website : Alexandre has programming skills, particularly in CSS, JS and HTML languages. He uses these skills to develop and improve the website, focusing on appearance and interaction with users.
  • Jawed Bensaih:
    • Website mockup design : Jawed is responsible for designing the website model. He ensures that web pages are properly structured and functional.
    • Creation of the program for the day to come in 2024 : Jawed is in charge of designing the detailed program for the day to come in 2024, taking into account activities, times and speakers. He ensures that the program is organized in a logical and attractive way for the participants.
  • Farès Nassim:
    • Public Relations Officer : Farès is responsible for maintaining the group's public relations. He may be in charge of contacting the media, sponsors or other stakeholders to promote the event. He also manages external communication and can be the main point of contact for questions and inquiries.
    • Formatting of the editorial staff of the site : Farès is responsible for formatting the writing for the website. He ensures that the content is well presented, with a clear structure and an attractive layout. It can also check text consistency, grammar and spelling.
