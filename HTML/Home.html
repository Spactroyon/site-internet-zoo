From September 16 to October 13, 2024

Season Wilderness

Discover the fascinating world of Madagascar

What's new on the park

2 baby fossas :

Tuesday, May 9, the fossas Zanahari the female, and Berany, the male of the park gave birth to Kevin and Martha which are a male and a female. So they have a 3 month gestation and a 5 month weaning. They will only be visible in their habitat with their parents from September 16 until October 13, 2024. You will be able to participate in vaccinations and various health inspections. A presenter will explain everything in detail on site at the event.

5 Ring-tailed mongooses:

The mongooses will arrive on September 16 at the same time as the first outing of the fossas babies. They are from Sarrebucks in Germany and have between 3 and 7 years old. The zoo will add a habitat in addition in the madagascar area, see the map below. Health reviews are planned during the season.


For more details on the dates of animation, see the program below.

Mongoose extension

The park will go through a small extension to accommodate the mongooses in their enclosure. The work will last from July 17 to August 30 but access to the Madagascar area will always be open.
